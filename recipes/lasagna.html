Luxurious Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe is quick to make, fast to bake

This lasagna will leave you loving the luxurious pizza and spaghetti combo!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz.) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. of lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 oz.) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 1/2 lb. of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb. of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pt. of ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of lightly salted water. Add the noodles and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente, then drain.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown the beef and season with the salt and pepper, then drain. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and the garlic and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the mozzarella, the cheddar, and the ricotta; stir well. In a 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture, and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy!
